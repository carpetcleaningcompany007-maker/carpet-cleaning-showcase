Meet Donna and the team

A reliable, friendly local service built on consistency, trust and doing the job properly every time.

Donna has spent years helping families, landlords and local businesses across Shrewsbury keep their properties clean, fresh and welcoming. Her approach is straightforward: turn up when promised, work carefully and leave the place looking and feeling better than when she arrived.
