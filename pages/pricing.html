Why Does The Price Change?

The cheaper options remove parts of the professional process. The Professional Deep Clean costs more because it uses stronger equipment, better chemistry, more labour, and a deeper cleaning method.

1. Extraction Machine

The machine affects cleaning power, heat, extraction strength and drying performance.

Professional Deep Clean

Enforcer 400 Professional Extraction System

  • 400 PSI hot water extraction
  • Built in heating up to 60°C
  • More powerful vacuum motors
  • Stronger extraction and better soil removal
  • Flagship professional carpet cleaning machine
Budget option

Airflex Mini

  • 220 PSI
  • No built in heater
  • Smaller unit
  • Less powerful vacuum motors
  • Suitable for lighter soil levels
2. Cleaning Solution

Targeted pre spray costs more because the product is better, the process takes longer, and the chemical has time to work.

Professional Deep Clean

Targeted Pre Spray Treatment

  • Applied separately before cleaning
  • Higher performance cleaning chemistry
  • Breaks down dirt, grease, traffic lanes and stains before rinsing
  • Allows dwell time
  • More labour intensive because each carpet is pre treated properly
Budget option

In Tank Detergent

  • Added directly into the machine rinse tank
  • Faster process
  • Lower chemical cost
  • No dwell time
  • Designed to self neutralise in the carpet rather than being rinsed away like targeted pre spray
3. Agitation

Agitation is one of the biggest differences between a basic clean and a proper deep clean.

Professional Deep Clean

CRB or Rotary Agitation

  • Scrubs deep into the carpet fibres
  • Helps lift the pile
  • Releases trapped soil
  • Improves the final result
  • Especially useful in walkways and traffic lanes
Budget option

No Mechanical Agitation

  • Wand extraction only
  • Quicker process
  • Less labour on each carpet
  • Suitable for lightly used carpets
4. Steam Clean System

Higher heat improves cleaning performance, especially on greasy or heavily used carpets.

Professional Deep Clean

Fusion Heat Bomb Steam System

  • Superheated hot water and steam
  • Can reach around 220°F
  • Helps cut through grease, grime and heavy soiling
  • Commercial upgrade equipment costing around £1,600 new
Budget option

Standard Heated Extraction

  • No Fusion Heat Bomb attached
  • Lower cleaning temperature
  • Fine for lighter soil levels

Read the lowest reviews carefully.

Every business can occasionally get a difficult customer. One poor review on its own does not always tell the full story. But if you see repeated complaints about price changes, poor workmanship, missed appointments, pressure selling or customers being dismissed, that is a warning sign.

Be careful if a company repeatedly replies to bad reviews by saying they have no record of the customer or do not recognise the job. Sometimes this may be genuine, but if it appears again and again, it can be a red flag.
