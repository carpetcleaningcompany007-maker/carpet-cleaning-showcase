Standard Clean

Prices start at £30 Per Room

This is our entry level clean. It is basically a spray and extract clean.

Detergent is added directly into the solution tank of the machine. The wand then sprays hot water mixed with detergent onto the carpet and extracts it back out at the same time.

This is a fast, simple clean with less time in the property and less disruption for the customer.

This is the type of low cost carpet clean commonly advertised all over the internet. The difference with us is that we are telling you exactly what you are getting for your money before you book.

Limitations

  • The detergent is designed to remain in the carpet. It is not a full rinse out process.

Every business can occasionally get a difficult customer. One poor review on its own does not always tell the full story. But if you see repeated complaints about price changes, poor workmanship, missed appointments, pressure selling or customers being dismissed, that is a warning sign.

Be careful if a company repeatedly replies to bad reviews by saying they have no record of the customer or do not recognise the job. Sometimes this may be genuine, but if it appears again and again, it can be a red flag.
