Process

Best for

This is where the cleaning process becomes very different.

We apply a targeted professional pre spray directly to the carpet and allow it to dwell. This softens dirt, grease and soil attached to the fibres.

We then use a counter rotating brush machine to scrub deep into the carpet pile. This lifts flattened fibres, opens the pile, and helps break dirt away from the carpet fibres.

The CRB machine lifts the carpet fibres, opens the pile and breaks dirt away from the fibres. This makes the hot water extraction stage far more effective.

Once the soil has been loosened and broken away, we rinse and extract using hot water extraction. This makes the extraction stage much more effective and gives the carpet that properly cleaned and rejuvenated look.

Every business can occasionally get a difficult customer. One poor review on its own does not always tell the full story. But if you see repeated complaints about price changes, poor workmanship, missed appointments, pressure selling or customers being dismissed, that is a warning sign.

Be careful if a company repeatedly replies to bad reviews by saying they have no record of the customer or do not recognise the job. Sometimes this may be genuine, but if it appears again and again, it can be a red flag.
