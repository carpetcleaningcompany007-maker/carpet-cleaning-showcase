Our three cleaning options

Each option uses professional equipment. The difference is in how much time, chemistry and agitation is invested into your carpets.

Tier one
Budget clean
First room about £45, then about £30 for each extra room.

This is the classic internet budget clean. It matches the type of thirty pound cleans that are advertised everywhere online. Anything at this price or cheaper will usually follow this method.

A strong in tank detergent is mixed with hot water and applied through the wand. The same solution is then extracted back out. Because the detergent is not separately rinsed, a small amount stays in the carpet.

It can give a simple freshen on short pile carpets but it is not a deep clean and it does not include targeted pre spray or mechanical agitation.

Budget refresh
Tier two
Enzyme clean
First room about £65, then about £35 for each extra room.

This option replaces the basic in tank detergent with a targeted pre spray that is applied directly into the fibres. It is designed to tackle food spills, drink marks, body oils, general soil and light pet related contamination.

The solution is given proper dwell time so that the enzymes can break down the contamination and release it from the pile. Hot steam extraction then flushes everything out and leaves the carpet cleaner and fresher.

You know you are getting more time and more focused chemistry than a basic budget clean, without going all the way to full restoration.

Family and pets
Tier three
Deep restoration clean
First room about £75, then about £45 for each extra room.

This is the full restoration method and the service behind the majority of my five star reviews. It is designed to bring carpets as close as possible to their best condition.

A specialist pre spray is applied and given time to work. A counter rotating brush then scrubs from both directions, lifting compacted soil and rejuvenating the pile so the carpet looks and feels new again.

Finally everything is rinsed out with high temperature steam extraction, often close to one hundred and twenty degrees, which cuts through grease and heavy traffic lanes and leaves the fibres brighter, softer and noticeably cleaner.
