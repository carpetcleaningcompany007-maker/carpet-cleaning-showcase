How the clean works

A simple method used in homes and workplaces so you know what to expect on the day.

Step one
Survey and vacuum

Short walk through, fibre check and a full dry vacuum to remove loose soil and grit before any solution is applied.

Step two
Pre spray and CRB agitation

Cleaning solution is chosen for the fibre type, then worked into the pile with a counter rotating brush to lift flattened fibres.

Step three
Hot water rinse and finish

High heat extraction with a neutralising rinse removes soil and residues so carpets dry clean and soft without sticky marks.
