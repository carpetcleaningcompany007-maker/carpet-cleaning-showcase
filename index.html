Our three cleaning options

Each option uses professional equipment. The difference is in how much time, chemistry and agitation is invested into your carpets.

Tier one
Budget clean
First room £45, then £30 for each extra room.

This is the classic internet budget clean. It matches the simple thirty pound cleans that are advertised everywhere online. Anything this cheap will usually follow this method.

A strong in tank detergent is mixed with hot water and applied through the wand. This is a detergent rinse and the cleaning solution is designed to stay inside the carpet rather than being fully rinsed out.

It can give a simple freshen on short pile carpets but it is not a deep clean and it does not include targeted pre spray or mechanical agitation.

Tier two
Enzyme clean
First room £65, then £35 for each extra room.

This option replaces the basic in tank detergent with a targeted pre spray that is applied directly into the fibres. It is designed to tackle food spills, drink marks, body oils, general soil and light pet related contamination.

The solution is then given a dwell time so the enzymes can break down the dirt and contamination and release it from the pile. Hot steam extraction at around one hundred and twenty degrees then flushes everything out and leaves the carpet cleaner and fresher.

This method does not give the carpet that fluffy new look because no counter rotating brush is used during the process.

Most popular
Tier three
Professional Deep Clean
First room £75, then £45 for each extra room.

This is the full professional deep clean method and the service behind all of my five star reviews. It is designed to bring carpets as close as possible to their best condition.

A specialist pre spray is applied and given time to work. A counter rotating brush then scrubs from both directions, lifting compacted soil and rejuvenating the pile so the carpet looks and feels new again.

Finally everything is rinsed out with high pressure high temperature steam extraction at around one hundred and twenty degrees, which cuts through grease, heavy traffic lanes and leaves the fibres brighter, softer, noticeably cleaner and fully sterilised.
