Each package is tailored to a different level of soiling. The deeper the package, the more time, machinery and chemistry is invested into the carpet.

This is the basic package and very similar to the budget cleans you often see advertised online.

A standard in tank detergent is mixed with hot water and applied through the wand. The same solution is then extracted back out. The detergent is not separately rinsed away and a small amount is left in the carpet.

It can give a reasonable freshen up on carpet tiles and very short pile carpets but it is not a deep clean.

Typical use

  • Rooms that already look fairly clean and just need a quick freshen up
  • Low profile carpets and carpet tiles
  • Situations where budget is the main priority over maximum soil removal

This is the intermediate clean. Instead of a basic in tank detergent, a targeted enzyme or specialist pre spray is applied directly into the carpet fibres.

The solution is allowed proper dwell time so it can break down food spills, body oils, general soil and light pet related contamination. Specific spotting solutions can also be used on marks that need extra attention.

Process

  • Optional professional pre vacuum where needed
  • Targeted enzyme or tailored pre spray applied to the fibres
  • Dwell time so soils soften and release from the pile
  • Hot water extraction, often up to around 120°C, to flush out loosened soil and residues

When to choose this

Ideal for most lived in family rooms where there are regular spills, general grime and light odours but the carpets do not yet need full restoration.

This is the premium service and the method responsible for the majority of my five star reviews. It delivers the deepest clean and is designed for carpets that need full restoration rather than a simple freshen up.

Full professional pre vacuum

A powerful commercial pre vacuum is used to remove as much loose dry soil, grit and fine particles as possible. Getting this dry soil out first means the cleaning chemistry can work at maximum efficiency.

Targeted pre spray treatment

A specialist pre spray is applied to break down heavy soil, oils, grease and stubborn traffic lane contamination. It is allowed proper dwell time so deep down dirt can soften and release from the fibres.

Counter rotating brush agitation

The counter rotating brush machine scrubs the carpet from both directions, lifting impacted soil to the surface and restoring the pile. This is the part that really cleans the fibres and removes material that extraction alone cannot reach.

High temperature steam extraction

Once the carpet has been fully agitated, everything is flushed out with high temperature extraction, up to around 120°C. This cuts through grease and embedded contamination and leaves the fibres brighter and softer.

The result

A dramatic improvement in appearance, hygiene and texture. This service aims to bring carpets as close as possible to their original condition and is my most effective and most popular package.
